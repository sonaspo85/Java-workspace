Quiet Mode
﻿

Using the quiet mode

Turn off the rear-seat speakers to prevent rear-seat occupants from being disturbed while sleeping.

  1. Swipe the top of the screen down to open the Quick Control screen.
  2. From the Quick Control screen, press Quiet Mode.

The rear-seat speakers turn off, and the volumes of the front-seat speakers are adjusted appropriately.

  • To close quiet mode, press Quiet Mode again from the Quick Control screen.
